About Us

Do you love off-road vehicles? Want to drive one yourself or simply explore the beauty of Ližnjan and its surroundings with your family?

Buggy World is the right choice for you!

We offer brand new buggy vehicles with a capacity for 2 or 4 people. The vehicles are suitable for all age groups. A Category B driver's license is required to drive. Enjoy the natural beauty of Ližnjan, Medulin, and Cape Kamenjak with carefully selected routes and our guide.

In addition, we also offer individual rental of our buggies.

Hurry up, book your appointment in time and create unforgettable memories of your vacation!

Meet our Fleet

Our Buggy World fleet consists of brand new, modern, powerful, and reliable off-road buggies for two or four people, designed for maximum fun and safety on any terrain.

The vehicles are ideal for adrenaline rides, tourist routes, and exploring nature – whether you come alone, as a couple, or in a group.

Can-Am 700

A powerful and agile two-seater, perfect for couples or friends looking for an adrenaline ride. Its excellent maneuverability and stability allow for easy handling of demanding terrains, while safety belts and a roll cage ensure a safe experience. Ideal for fast and exciting routes.

Polaris RZR

The Polaris two-seater is a buggy with ultimate durability and performance, made for adrenaline junkies. It offers maximum control on challenging terrains and an intense off-road experience. Ideal for those seeking a combination of power, agility, and pure excitement in nature.

CF-Moto ZFORCE (4 seats)

Spacious and comfortable four-seater for families or groups of friends. It maintains the power and reliability needed for off-road adventures while offering more space for shared fun. Perfect for longer routes and exploration in good company, without compromising on safety and performance.

Catalog of Guided Routes

Experience a worry-free adventure with our professionally organized guided buggy tours. An experienced guide will lead you through the most attractive tracks – from gravel roads and forest paths to hidden viewpoints.

* Additional charge for riding in a four-seater (CF-Moto ZFORCE) is €30 per tour

Route 1 – Cape Kamenjak

Join us on a tour to Cape Kamenjak, the southernmost part of Istria with a total of 30 km of coastline, 30 bays, and 11 uninhabited islands of the Medulin archipelago. The tour includes a visit to the legendary Safari Beach Bar, where you can enjoy refreshing drinks, delicious food, or a swim in the sea during a one-hour break.

  • Duration3 hours
  • Price€210
  • Length30 km

Route 2 – Lighthouse and Beaches

On this route, we explore lush forests and discover beautiful, hard-to-reach beaches of Ližnjan and Šišan. We will stop at the Marlera lighthouse for photos and, if you wish, for a swim in the sea. Discover the beauty of the Istrian coast on a relaxed or exciting ride through nature.

  • Duration120 minutes
  • Price€170
  • Length30 km

Route 3 – Beach Tour

Embark on an exciting buggy adventure through the scenic landscapes of Ližnjan and Šišan. Gravel trails, untouched nature, and unforgettable sea views await you behind every corner. Adrenaline, nature, and freedom – all in one ride! Book today!

  • Duration60 minutes
  • Price€120
  • Length30 km
